About Us

Welcome to The Blue Academy Inter-house Sports Competition fundraising website. We are excited to be hosting this year's sports competition, and we're grateful for your support in making it a success.

Our goal is to provide an opportunity for our students to showcase their skills, build their confidence, and foster a sense of community within the school. The Inter-house Sports Competition is an important event in our school calendar, and we rely on the generous support of parents like you to help make it happen. Thank you for your support.
